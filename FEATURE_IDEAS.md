## âš™ï¸ Financial Automation Track

### 11. **Smart Contract-Based Budgeting**
- Set spending limits per category (payroll, operations, marketing)
- Automatic budget enforcement via smart contracts
- Alerts when approaching budget limits
- Use case: DAO treasury management, company budget controls

### 12. **Multi-Signature Payment Approvals**
- Require multiple signatures for large payments
- Configurable approval thresholds (e.g., >$10k requires 2/3 signatures)
- Role-based approval permissions
- Use case: Enterprise treasury, DAO governance, high-value transactions

### 13. **Automated Tax Withholding**
- Calculate and withhold taxes automatically
- Distribute net pay and send tax portion to tax authority wallet
- Multi-jurisdiction tax support
- Use case: Global payroll with automatic tax compliance

### 14. **Expense Reimbursement Automation**
- Employees submit expenses via app
- AI validates receipts and calculates reimbursement
- Automatic MNEE payment on approval
- Use case: Employee expense management and reimbursement

### 15. **Invoice Factoring & Early Payment**
- Businesses can sell invoices for immediate MNEE payment
- Smart contract manages invoice lifecycle
- Automatic payment on invoice due date
- Use case: Cash flow management for small businesses

### 16. **Yield Farming for Treasury**
- Automatically invest idle MNEE in DeFi protocols
- Earn yield while maintaining liquidity
- Automatic withdrawal when payments are due
- Use case: Optimize treasury returns while maintaining payment capability

### 17. **Cross-Chain Payment Bridge**
- Bridge MNEE payments to other chains (if MNEE supports it)
- Automatic conversion and routing
- Multi-chain payroll support
- Use case: Global teams preferring different blockchains

### 18. **Payment Scheduling with Conditions**
- Schedule payments that only execute if conditions are met
- Example: Pay employee only if they complete training
- Smart contract-based condition checking
- Use case: Conditional payroll, milestone-based payments

### 19. **Automated Currency Hedging**
- Automatically hedge currency exposure for international payments
- Convert MNEE to local stablecoins when needed
- Minimize FX risk for global payroll
- Use case: Multi-currency payroll with automatic hedging

### 20. **Payment Analytics & Forecasting**
- AI-powered payment analytics dashboard
- Predict future payment needs based on historical data
- Cash flow forecasting and optimization
- Use case: Financial planning and treasury management
